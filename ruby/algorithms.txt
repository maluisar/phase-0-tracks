#Elevator

1. Ask what floor do you want to go to.
2. If desired floor minus current floor greater than 0 press up button. Otherwise press down button.
3. Wait for elavator.
4. Enter elevator
5. Press desired floor button.
6. If current floor equal to desired floor, exit elevator.
